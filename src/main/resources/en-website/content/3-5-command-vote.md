title=Vote Command
title_en=vote
~~~~~~
Parameters:
Person or People - the list of telegram username(s) that are separated with space.
After starting the [election](#startElection) or [final election](#startFinalElection), players should announce their vote by entering the following command. E.g. for voting for the user “Alice”, you should enter:
<pre><code>/vote Alice</code></pre>
or for vote for “Alice” and “Bob”, enter:
<pre><code>/vote Alice ‌Bob</code></pre>
or to vote for no one, simply enter:
<pre><code>/vote nobody</code></pre>
