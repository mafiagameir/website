title=Start Command
title_en=start
~~~~~~
Parameters:
-number of citizens
-number of mafia
existence of detective - 0 or 1
existence of doctor - 0 or 1

Sample: for starting a game with 8 citizens, 2 mafias, 1 detective and without doctor, you should enter:

<pre><code>/start 8 2 1 0</code></pre>
